FEATURES

What Scrubbed does for you

01

The Premed Vault — your application, all in one place

Log your activities, clinical experiences, research, volunteering, and personal statement into a structured profile. The Vault is the foundation SecondaryAI draws from — so every outline it generates is grounded in what you've actually done, not generic filler.

The Premed Vault
02

School-specific essay outlines, built from your profile

SecondaryAI reads each school's secondary prompt alongside your Vault and generates a personalized outline — which experiences to highlight, how to frame your narrative, and what this specific school is looking for. Not a draft. A blueprint that's actually yours.

SecondaryAI outlines
03

Portfolio intelligence — see your entire cycle at once

Scrubbed holds all your essays simultaneously and flags what you can't catch yourself: the same anecdote appearing across six schools that will both read you, strong credentials never mentioned in any essay, and voice drift between essays written weeks apart. No spreadsheet can do this.

Portfolio health
04

Smart timing — know when to submit

Secondary essays at most schools are reviewed on a rolling basis. Scrubbed tracks how long each secondary has been sitting in your queue and surfaces prioritization guidance — so you're submitting where it matters most, before the window quietly closes.
